# Style Profile

Status: for review

## Style Name

Modern warm magical-realist family comic

## Source Input

User requested a warm, moving modern mother-and-child daily-life story that uses GPT-image as the single high-quality image model. The style should maximize GPT-image strengths: emotional lighting, cinematic framing, soft realism, expressive faces, cloth detail, and gentle magical realism.

## Visual Keywords

- modern Chinese family apartment
- warm morning kitchen
- school uniform fabric detail
- tiny embroidered golden star
- rain at school gate
- desk lamp at midnight
- quiet city sidewalk
- graduation hall light beam
- city night and home window lights
- gentle magical realism
- cinematic picture-book comic

## Color Palette

Warm cream, soft beige, honey gold, school-uniform navy, rainy blue gray, muted city purple, small gold highlights from the star motif.

## Line And Texture

Soft semi-realistic comic illustration with painterly texture, delicate cloth folds, natural skin tones, expressive but restrained faces, clean background detail, not flat cartoon, not hyperreal photo.

## Camera Language

Vertical 9:16 cinematic panels. Use intimate medium shots, close-ups of hands and fabric, over-the-shoulder views, quiet wide shots for emotional transitions, and shallow depth of field where useful.

## Composition Rules

Each panel should have one clear emotional subject. The golden star motif should appear directly or symbolically in every panel. Keep the mother and child visually recognizable across panels.

## Lighting Rules

Use warm interior light for home scenes, cool rain light for school scenes, soft desk lamp light for night scenes, and blue-purple city light with tiny gold highlights for the adult ending.

## Era And World Rules

Contemporary Chinese city life. No fantasy creatures, no horror, no old-era clothing, no sci-fi UI. The magical element is subtle: the star can glow gently, but the world remains realistic.

## Negative Style Rules

No horror, no melodrama tears, no exaggerated anime expressions, no chibi style, no glossy idol poster look, no unreadable clutter, no watermark, no logo, no visible brand names, no large readable Chinese text inside the image.
