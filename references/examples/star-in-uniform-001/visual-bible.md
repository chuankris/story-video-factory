# Visual Bible

Status: for review

## Episode

妈妈把星星缝进我的校服

## Visual Thesis

A warm modern family comic where a tiny golden embroidered star becomes the visual proof of love. The story should feel quiet, cinematic, and emotionally luminous rather than sentimental or exaggerated.

## Core Visual Motif

The tiny golden star.

Rules:

- It appears as sleeve embroidery in school panels.
- It may glow subtly, but never turns the world into fantasy.
- It becomes a backpack patch in the adult city panel.
- It can be echoed by window lights, streetlights, stage lights, and city stars.

## Setting

Modern Chinese city and family apartment:

- small warm kitchen
- school gate in rain
- girl's bedroom at night
- quiet sidewalk to school
- home entryway during argument
- graduation hall
- big city night street

## Character Continuity

Mother:

- gentle modern Chinese mother
- cream cardigan/apron
- tied dark hair
- warm tired eyes

girl:

- navy-and-white school uniform
- small golden star on sleeve
- sensitive, slightly stubborn expression

Adult girl:

- practical city outfit
- backpack with golden star patch
- same gentle eyes as girl

## Image Style

Modern warm magical-realist family comic. Soft semi-realistic painterly illustration, cinematic vertical 9:16, warm light, delicate fabric detail, expressive restrained faces.

## Text Policy

No large readable text inside images. Avoid visible brand names, logos, school names, and UI text. Captions are provided outside the image through `script.json`.


## Bilingual Review Rule

For GPT-image runs, use English `prompt` for generation quality and Chinese `prompt_zh` for human review. The Chinese description must describe the same visual intent, characters, style, and constraints as the English prompt.
## Provider Plan

Single-model run with GPT-image.

- Generate 10 production panels if story and prompts are approved.
- The first approved panel can serve as a style anchor for later reference.
- If character drift is severe, pause and decide whether to generate a character sheet before continuing.

## Review Notes

Please review before image generation:

- Is the star motif strong enough?
- The protagonist is fixed as a cute modern Chinese girl; please review whether her age, temperament, and visual continuity feel right.
- Should the final panel show the mother physically, or only the phone message and home lights?
