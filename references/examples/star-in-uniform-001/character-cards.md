# Character Cards

## Character: Mother

Role in story: quiet, loving mother who expresses care through small acts rather than speeches.

Age impression: early 30s in early panels, late 40s in the final memory/ending if shown.

Face shape: soft oval face, gentle eyes, natural tiredness, warm expression.

Hair: dark brown or black hair tied loosely at home; simple low ponytail or soft bun.

Costume: simple cream cardigan or soft home sweater in morning scenes; muted apron when cooking or sewing; practical modern clothing, no luxury styling.

Signature object or mark: sewing needle, golden thread, folded school uniform, warm hands.

Temperament: patient, quiet, slightly tired but emotionally steady.

Common posture or gesture: leaning over fabric, smoothing wrinkles, touching her daughter's sleeve, sitting under a desk lamp.

Forbidden changes: do not make her glamorous, cold, wealthy-looking, fantasy-like, or visibly elderly in school-year scenes.

Reference assets: none yet.

Prompt block:

```text
The mother is a modern Chinese woman in her early 30s, gentle oval face, dark hair tied loosely, soft cream cardigan or apron, warm patient eyes, quiet tired tenderness, practical and natural, not glamorous. She lovingly cares for her daughter.
```

## Character: Girl

Role in story: daughter growing from dependent elementary-school girl to independent young woman.

Age impression: 10-12 years old in school panels; early 20s in final city panel.

Face shape: cute round youthful face in childhood, soft cheeks, clear sensitive eyes; adult version keeps the same gentle eyes.

Hair: simple black bob or low ponytail in childhood, neat school-day look; adult version shoulder-length natural black hair.

Costume: navy-and-white modern school uniform in childhood; simple coat or hoodie with backpack in adult city scene.

Signature object or mark: small golden star embroidered on sleeve, later moved to backpack.

Temperament: cute, sensitive, a little stubborn while growing up, ultimately tender and reflective.

Common posture or gesture: looking down at sleeve, hiding sleeve, holding backpack strap, standing alone on a sidewalk.

Forbidden changes: do not change her into a fantasy character, idol-style teen, exaggerated anime girl, or unrelated adult.

Reference assets: none yet.

Prompt block:

```text
The daughter is a cute modern Chinese elementary-school girl, about 10-12 years old, round youthful face, clear sensitive eyes, simple black bob or low ponytail, navy-and-white school uniform, a little stubborn but kind. A tiny golden star is embroidered on her sleeve.
```

## Character: Adult Daughter

Role in story: grown version of the girl, leaving home but carrying the same love forward.

Age impression: early 20s.

Face shape: resembles the childhood girl with the same gentle eyes, more mature and calm.

Hair: shoulder-length natural black hair, understated and practical.

Costume: practical city outfit, dark coat or hoodie, backpack with the golden star patch.

Signature object or mark: golden star patch on backpack.

Temperament: independent, quiet, homesick but brave.

Common posture or gesture: holding suitcase handle, touching backpack star, looking at city lights.

Forbidden changes: do not make her fashion-model-like, cyberpunk, wealthy-looking, or unrelated to the childhood girl.

Prompt block:

```text
The adult daughter is a modern Chinese young woman in her early 20s, calm gentle eyes resembling the childhood girl, shoulder-length natural black hair, practical city clothes, backpack with the same tiny golden star patch, independent but quietly homesick.
```
