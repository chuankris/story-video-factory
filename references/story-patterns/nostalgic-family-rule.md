# Story Pattern: Nostalgic Family Rule

Status: validated by `episodes/five-rings-home-2006-001` (`响五声就好`)

## Purpose

Use this pattern for warm, realistic family comics where the emotional hook comes from a small repeated rule inside an older everyday life context.

This pattern is especially suitable for Douyin image carousels because it gives each page a clear reveal while keeping the ending quiet and shareable.

## Core Formula

```text
Past era constraint + family money constraint + private rule + repeated ritual + one broken rule + quiet final line
```

The audience should understand the rule by page 4 or 5. The climax happens when the rule is broken.

## Why It Works

- A rule is easier to remember than a general emotion.
- Repetition builds expectation without needing complicated plot.
- A small object can carry the whole story.
- The parent does not need to explain love; their behavior explains it.
- The final line can be gentle instead of dramatic.

## Example: 响五声就好

```text
2006 long-distance phone fees + ordinary family budget + five rings mean safe + mother counts every Friday + sixth ring + "妈今天不数铃"
```

Opening hook:

```text
2006年，我妈从来不接我的电话。
```

Payoff:

```text
我只说：妈，我有点想家。她说：那你慢慢说，妈今天不数铃。
```

## Page Structure

Recommended 10-12 panels:

1. Counterintuitive hook: a behavior that sounds cold or strange.
2. Era setup: show why the rule exists.
3. Family setup: ordinary objects and money constraint.
4. Rule statement: make the private agreement clear.
5. Rule consequence: what each side should do.
6. Repetition montage: weeks or months pass.
7. Parent alone with the ritual.
8. Child alone with unsaid words.
9. The rule breaks.
10. Parent responds instantly.
11. Quiet final line.
12. Optional afterglow or object close-up.

## Visual Ingredients

Use concrete era objects:

- Landline, phone card, coins, bus card, meal ticket, old umbrella, thermos, enamel cup, patched bag.
- Warm home light versus cold outside/campus/station light.
- Parent's hands and small chores.
- Avoid glamorous styling. Use ordinary clothes and worn surfaces.

## Prompt Reminder

For parent realism, place this near the subject description:

```text
clearly middle-aged 45-50 year old Chinese parent, ordinary county-town worker/parent, worn hands, practical clothes, tired gentle eyes, not glamorous, not young
```

For older-era control:

```text
no smartphones, no QR codes, no modern LED screens, no contemporary branding
```

## Variations

Father version:

- Bus card means he arrived safely.
- A father never enters the university gate.
- A monthly parcel has hidden money in old clothes.

Mother version:

- A missed call code.
- A meal ticket rule.
- An old umbrella left at the school gate.

Teacher version:

- A red pen mark used as a private encouragement rule.
- A classroom light that is always left on until the last student leaves.

## Acceptance Criteria

A story using this pattern is ready for outline review when:

- The private rule can be explained in one sentence.
- The rule is tied to a concrete object.
- The parent has a visible repeated action.
- The final broken-rule moment changes the emotional meaning of the whole story.
- The ending can fit into one quiet sentence.
