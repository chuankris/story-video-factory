# Direct Video Mode

Status: later phase

Direct video mode uses video-generation providers for clips or full scenes. It is intentionally behind pure comic mode because it is more expensive, less deterministic, and harder to review.

## When To Consider

- A hook or reveal needs motion that still images cannot carry.
- The story already has an approved script and visual bible.
- Provider quota is available.

## Current Rule

Treat direct video as an extension, not the default. Do not spend video quota until the user explicitly chooses this mode.
