# Character Cards

## Son

- Chinese male, 18-19 years old in 2006.
- First-generation college freshman from an ordinary county-town family.
- Slim build, short black hair, clean but slightly worn clothes.
- Typical outfit: faded blue or gray jacket, plain T-shirt or sweater, canvas backpack, inexpensive sneakers.
- Personality: sensible, proud, careful with money, does not easily say he misses home.
- Visual anchors: long-distance phone card, coins in palm, patched canvas suitcase, thin winter scarf in later panels.

## Mother

- Chinese woman, early to mid 40s in 2006.
- Ordinary county-town mother, practical, restrained, deeply attentive.
- Hair tied back loosely; simple sweater, apron, cloth sleeves or old cardigan.
- Expression: tired eyes, gentle mouth, often holding back worry.
- Visual anchors: landline telephone, vegetable basin, old wall calendar, enamel cup, needlework or folded sweater.

## Family Home

- Small modest home in a county town.
- Cream-colored landline on a small wooden table.
- Warm dim bulb, old thermos, enamel cup, wall calendar, vegetable basket, chipped table edge.
- No modern smart devices.

## University Campus

- 2006 public university environment.
- Iron bunk bed dormitory, shared corridor, campus grocery store public phone, green phone booth, concrete walls.
- Faded posters may exist but should not contain readable text.
