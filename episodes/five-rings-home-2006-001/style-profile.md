# Style Profile: 2006 Long-Distance Phone Nostalgia

## Intent

A restrained, warm, realistic comic style for a 2006 Chinese family story. The images should feel like pages from an old memory: ordinary objects, low-income household details, and gentle emotion rather than dramatic crying.

## Visual Keywords

- 2006 China, ordinary county town family, first-generation college student.
- Warm tungsten indoor light, blue-gray winter exterior, faded film grain.
- Soft semi-realistic painterly comic linework, expressive but not exaggerated faces.
- Old landline telephone, long-distance phone card, public phone booth, campus grocery store phone, iron dorm bed, enamel basin, old wall calendar, thermos, coins, patched canvas suitcase.
- Vertical 9:16, mobile-readable composition, clean upper-left caption space.

## Palette

- Home: warm ochre, ivory wall, old wood brown, muted red calendar, dim yellow bulb.
- Campus: faded green, blue-gray evening, concrete wall gray, weak fluorescent light.
- Winter turning point: cold blue shadows contrasted with warm telephone booth or home light.

## Line And Texture

- Pencil-like sketch lines, watercolor wash, light paper texture.
- Slight imperfection is welcome: frayed cloth, chipped table edge, faded phone card.
- Avoid glossy commercial poster look.

## Emotional Rules

- The mother is never melodramatic. She waits, counts, exhales, answers.
- The son is not childish. He is newly independent, proud, lonely, and careful with money.
- Let objects carry emotion: coins, phone card, receiver, wall clock, vegetables waiting in a basin.

## Text Policy

Do not render story captions inside the generated image. Captions are added locally using `douyin-msyh-top` Microsoft YaHei style.

Avoid logos, brand names, readable school names, modern smartphones, QR codes, app UI, LED screens, or contemporary objects.

## Provider Notes

GPT-image main route: use English prompts with specific era details and restrained emotion.

MiniMax optional side test: only after one GPT-image baseline is approved, test one phone/character interaction panel for character consistency.
