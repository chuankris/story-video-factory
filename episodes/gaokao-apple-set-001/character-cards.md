# Character Cards

## Daughter / Candidate

- Role: gaokao student, first-person narrator.
- Age impression: 17-18.
- Face shape: soft round face, slightly tired but bright eyes.
- Hair: simple black ponytail or short tied hair, no elaborate styling.
- Costume: plain school uniform or simple T-shirt, practical backpack.
- Signature object: cracked old phone, admission ticket, one apple in backpack.
- Temperament: considerate, a little self-conscious, quietly stubborn.
- Common posture: looking down, hiding embarrassment with a smile, later holding apple close.
- Forbidden changes: do not make her wealthy-looking, flashy, cynical, or bitter.

## Mother

- Role: ordinary working mother.
- Age impression: late 30s to mid 40s.
- Face shape: gentle, slightly tired, warm eyes.
- Hair: simple tied-back black hair, a few loose strands.
- Costume: home apron, plain shirt, practical cloth shoes on exam day.
- Signature object: plastic bag with apple, wet shoes, dishwashing hands.
- Temperament: careful, loving, not good at big speeches.
- Common posture: standing slightly behind, watching quietly, pretending things are casual.
- Forbidden changes: do not make her pitiful caricature, overly dramatic, or saint-like.
