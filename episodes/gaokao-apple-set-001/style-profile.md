# Style Profile

style_name: handwritten_douyin_story
source_input: Douyin-native handwritten story comics with warm paper background, black handwritten text, colored brush highlight strokes, mixed panel layouts, and high readability on mobile.

visual_keywords:
- warm gaokao diary comic
- hand-drawn sketch lines
- paper texture
- soft watercolor wash
- expressive but restrained faces
- ordinary Chinese family home
- realistic school life

color_palette:
- warm ivory paper
- pencil black linework
- muted red apple highlight
- pale green apple highlight
- soft blue-gray classroom shadows
- rainy exam-day gray

line_and_texture:
- loose hand-drawn outlines
- visible pencil/ink wobble
- watercolor-like shadows
- mild paper grain
- not photorealistic

camera_language:
- mix of single scenes, two-panel contrast, three-panel detail, four-panel sequence, and object collage
- leave intentional blank zones for text overlay
- keep key faces away from right-side Douyin buttons
- keep key text away from bottom UI

composition_rules:
- top 25%-35% reserved for handwritten story text when possible
- bottom 22% should not contain essential text
- right 18% should avoid faces and key props
- panel layouts should be clear at mobile size

lighting_rules:
- soft practical home lighting
- classroom daylight
- rainy exam-site gray with warm emotional accents

era_and_world_rules:
- contemporary China, gaokao season
- ordinary family, no luxury home
- no brand logos, no readable school names
- avoid attacking classmates who own devices

negative_style_rules:
- no model-generated final Chinese story text
- no mockery of poverty
- no melodramatic crying closeups
- no luxury-product worship
- no bottom caption card

provider_notes:
- Generate clean image with blank text zones.
- Final Chinese story text is added locally after generation.
- Prompt English for generation; keep prompt_zh for review.
